Campaign — 2020

The Project

Create a campaign for the Sustainable Business Conference 2020. Design a wordmark logo, brochure, pocket folder and website for the event.

My Role

The design was created with the idea of sustainability in mind, but also suitable for a corporate setting through the use of minimalism and colours of bright greens and deep blues. The wordmark was designed with the idea of "continous cycle," using a half circle as representation.
