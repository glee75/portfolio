CUPNOODL​ES Museum

Branding — 2021

The Project

Choose a cultural institution or museum and rebrand it, using the mediums of posters, banners, billboards and merchandise.

My Role

I chose the CUPNOODLES Museum for this project due to my interest in developing ideas for a museum focused on one of my favourite foods. The redesigned logo and promotional materials were designed with minimalism in mind to emphasize the imagery and maintains the same red from the original design for familiarity of original CUPNOODLE packaging.
