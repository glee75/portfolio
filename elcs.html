Marketing Campaign — 2021

The Project

In a team of 7, develop a marketing campaign within 6 weeks for the non-profit organization, Experience Life Community Services (ELCS) and explore rebranding options to give the organization a new and fresh look.

Our team produced a redesigned logo, website, social media pages (Facebook, Instagram, Youtube), a video featuring ELCS participants, and multiple pieces of branded material (i.e buttons, tshirts, water bottles, banners, van)

My Role

I took on the role of an Interviewer/Assistant Designer with the ELCS Project. My primary role was to collect research, write progress reports, develop questions and interview the Managing Director of ELCS Programming as well as the participants in-person and virtually. My secondary role was focused on developing new logo concepts, refining the redesigned logo and providing visuals for social media posts.
